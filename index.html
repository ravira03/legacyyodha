Why Choose Us?

A woman with a Voice is by definition a strong woman. But the search to find that voice can be remarkably difficult. We Net Tribe ensures every woman's voices are heard and heeded, but never overlooked or ignored. The experts in the team lead the Tribe with their expertise in different fields from Education to Spirituality. The beginners in the team are given the opportunity to learn, expand and grow within and outside the community The service team takes care of underprivileged women to start small scale business with the support and new business ideas from the Tribe A part of the Revenue generated in the Tribe gets its path in the education of the underprivileged students

Founder

The purpose behind the initiative of the tribe The mystery of human existence lies not just in staying alive, but in finding something to live for. My hunt for the purpose of my life’s mystery started way back in college. Lacking proper guidance, exploring a lot of streams for decades, life not only taught me the harsh reality but made me feel the importance of the right guidance at the right time. Pressurizing myself personally, professionally, and economically, I decided not to give upon or compromise for anything smaller .. Gradually I realized, no matter what one does, regardless of failure or success, the experience is a form of success in itself. Learning and growing wiser is what makes us win. Deep down it has always been in my conscious thoughts to create a space for togetherness and awareness for people who look upon someone trustworthy. Through the ups and downs of running, I found a new perspective in life. The sufferings, learnings, failures, and success made me a stronger and happier person. After all, I started chasing my real Purpose of Life – To be benevolent, generous and share the wisdom gained. To spread strength and happiness and to fulfill my life’s soul purpose, We NET Tribe was given its formation This tribe of togetherness is for the personal development of all womanhood and students who need guidance and support in their journey. Love, light, peace, togetherness, and abundance; Poornima Swamy.
